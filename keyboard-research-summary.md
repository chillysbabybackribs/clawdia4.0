# Keyboard Research Summary

> Source: RTINGS.com | Compiled: 2026-03-21

A comparison of two top-rated low-profile wireless keyboards for office/typing use, based on RTINGS reviews.

---

## 🏆 Top Picks

### 1. Logitech MX Keys S

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Scissor-switch, low-profile, full-size (100%) |
| **Connectivity** | Bluetooth (up to 4 devices) or Logi BOLT USB receiver |
| **Backlighting** | Per-key white backlight with ambient light auto-adjustment |
| **RTINGS Rating** | Very good for office use |
| **Review Updated** | Feb 2026 (methodology v1.4.3) |

**Highlights:**
- ✅ Very quiet typing noise
- ✅ Spherical dish-shaped keycaps keep fingers centered, reduce typos
- ✅ Excellent build quality; comfortable low-profile design — no wrist rest needed
- ✅ Recommended by RTINGS for: Keyboards, Typing, Mac, Office, Programming, Low-Profile, Quiet
- ⚠️ Latency is decent for casual use but **not suitable for competitive gaming**
- ➕ Adds emoji key on function row vs. previous MX Keys model

**Buy:** [Amazon — B0BKVZK4TH](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0BKVZK4TH)
**Full Review:** [RTINGS](https://www.rtings.com/keyboard/reviews/logitech/mx-keys-s)

---

### 2. Keychron K5 Max

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Mechanical, low-profile, full-size (100%), aluminum case |
| **Switches** | Gateron Low Profile 2.0 — Brown (tactile) or Red (linear, quiet) |
| **Connectivity** | 2.4GHz USB receiver or Bluetooth (up to 3 devices) |
| **Backlighting** | Full RGB |
| **Polling Rate** | True 1000Hz (wired & 2.4GHz wireless) |
| **RTINGS Rating** | Great office keyboard |
| **Review Updated** | Apr 2025 (test bench Mar 2026, methodology v1.4.3) |

**Highlights:**
- ✅ Quiet typing noise
- ✅ Excellent build quality (aluminum case)
- ✅ Switches and stabilizers pre-lubed from factory
- ✅ Hot-swappable PCB — swap switches without soldering
- ✅ True 1000Hz polling rate — excellent latency over wired or 2.4GHz
- ✅ Web-based + downloadable software for remapping, macros, and backlight
- ✅ Recommended by RTINGS for: Office, Low-Profile — **zero listed cons for office use**
- ⚠️ Stock keycaps do **not** have shine-through legends (hard to read in dark rooms)

**Full Review:** [RTINGS](https://www.rtings.com/keyboard/reviews/keychron/k5-max-k1-max-k3-max-etc)
